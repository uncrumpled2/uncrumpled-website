Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Uncrumpled ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Email Address: When you sign up for our waitlist or participate in surveys, we collect your email address to send you updates about Uncrumpled.
  • Survey Responses: If you participate in our feedback surveys, we collect your responses to help improve our product.
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/region). This data is only collected if you accept analytics cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you updates about Uncrumpled's development and launch
  • Understand how visitors use our website to improve the user experience
  • Gather feedback to shape our product development
  • Respond to your inquiries

Cookies

We use cookies for analytics purposes only. When you first visit our site, you'll be asked whether you accept or decline analytics cookies. If you accept, Google Analytics will track anonymous usage data. If you decline, no analytics cookies will be set.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser's local storage and revisiting the site.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous website usage analytics. Google's Privacy Policy
  • Google Sheets: To store waitlist signups and survey responses securely.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Decline analytics cookies

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
