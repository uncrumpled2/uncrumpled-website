Finally, a frictionless way to organize your digital life.

The Problem

Context switching kills flow

Switching between apps and projects breaks concentration and costs precious mental energy.

Notes are scattered

Important information lives in dozens of places, making it hard to find when you need it most.

Setup takes forever

Reconstructing project setups—terminals, tabs, windows—is slow and repetitive work.

The Vision

One hotkey brings up context-specific notes

Switching projects automatically changes workspaces

Apps, terminals, and sessions can be snapshotted and restored
